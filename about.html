About Reno Water Damage Pros

Family-owned and Reno-based since 2015. We're your neighbors, and we care about this community.

Our Story

Reno Water Damage Pros started in 2015 when founder Mike Stevens experienced a burst pipe in his own South Reno home. The national franchise company that responded took three hours to arrive, charged premium prices, and treated him like just another invoice. Mike, who had 15 years of construction experience, saw an opportunity to do things differently.

He wanted to build a local company that actually cared about Reno families. A company that responded fast, charged fair prices, and treated every home like it was their own. Starting with one truck and working nights and weekends, Mike built Reno Water Damage Pros into the area's most trusted restoration company.

Today, we operate five fully-equipped trucks, employ 18 trained technicians, and have completed over 1,200 restoration projects throughout Washoe County. We're still family-owned, still local, and still committed to the values we started with: fast response, honest service, and exceptional work.

Why Choose a Local Company?

When water damage strikes, you have a choice between national franchises and local companies. Here's why local matters:

  • We're actually here. National companies often subcontract to local crews you've never heard of. When you call us, you get our team. We live here, our trucks are based here, and we can respond faster than anyone.
  • We understand Reno. We know which neighborhoods flood during storms, when pipes freeze, and what building codes apply to local properties. This local knowledge makes us more efficient and effective.
  • We care about our reputation. Our business depends on Reno referrals and reviews. National franchises can afford a few bad reviews. We can't, and we don't want to. Every job matters to us.
  • We're accountable. If you have a problem, you can reach Mike directly. Try getting a national franchise owner on the phone. We stand behind our work because our name is on it.

Our Certifications and Training

All our technicians receive extensive training and hold industry certifications:

  • IICRC Water Damage Restoration Technician (WRT) certification
  • IICRC Applied Structural Drying (ASD) certification
  • IICRC Mold Remediation certification
  • OSHA safety training for hazardous materials
  • Ongoing manufacturer training on restoration equipment
  • Annual continuing education to maintain certifications

We invest heavily in training because it directly impacts the quality of our work. A properly trained technician knows how to find hidden moisture, set up drying equipment efficiently, and prevent mold growth. This expertise saves you money and prevents future problems.

Our Equipment

We use professional-grade equipment that delivers superior results:

  • Truck-mounted water extractors (far more powerful than portable units)
  • Commercial dehumidifiers (150+ pints per day capacity)
  • High-velocity air movers (thousands of CFM airflow)
  • Thermal imaging cameras (detect hidden moisture)
  • Calibrated moisture meters (accurate drying verification)
  • HEPA air scrubbers (mold spore removal)
  • Specialty hardwood floor drying systems

This equipment represents a significant investment, but it's essential for thorough, efficient restoration. We maintain and calibrate all equipment regularly to ensure peak performance.
